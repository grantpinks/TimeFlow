# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Overview
This document provides answers to common questions about TimeFlow.

## General Questions

### Q: What is TimeFlow?
A: TimeFlow is a productivity application designed to help users manage their tasks, schedules, and more effectively.

### Q: What platforms does TimeFlow support?
A: TimeFlow currently supports web and mobile platforms.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?
A: Please ensure you are using the correct credentials. If you are still unable to log in, try resetting your password or contacting support.

### Q: My calendar isn't syncing. How can I fix this?
A: Check your internet connection and ensure that TimeFlow has the necessary permissions to access your calendar. You can also try re-linking your calendar account in the settings.
