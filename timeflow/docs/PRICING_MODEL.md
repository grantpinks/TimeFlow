# TimeFlow Pricing Model

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-03

## 1. Overview & Philosophy

This document outlines the official pricing model for TimeFlow. Our strategy is a "smarter freemium" model designed to provide significant value at every level, encouraging user acquisition with a strong free plan while offering a clear and compelling path to upgrade.

The tiers are designed to align with the needs of our target users: ambitious students, busy professionals, and power users who want to fully automate their productivity.

## 2. Pricing Tiers

We offer three main tiers. All paid tiers include an option for annual billing, which provides a "2 months free" discount.

### **Tier 1: Starter (Free)**

*   **Price:** $0
*   **Target Audience:** Individuals new to productivity tools, students, or users with light scheduling needs.
*   **Goal:** Introduce the core "magic" of AI-assisted scheduling and create a path to long-term user engagement.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Core Scheduling:** Basic drag-and-drop scheduling, simple task creation.
    *   **AI Assistant:** Reactive AI with a limit of **200 Flow Credits per month.**
    *   **Integrations:** Sync with **1** calendar.
    *   **Habit Tracking:** Simple daily/weekly reminders.

### **Tier 2: Pro ($5/month)**

*   **Price:** $5 per month, or $50 per year.
*   **Target Audience:** Professionals and students who need more organizational power and a more helpful AI assistant.
*   **Goal:** Provide a significant productivity boost at an accessible price point.
*   **Features:**
    *   Everything in **Starter**, plus:
    *   **Core Scheduling:** Advanced features (subtasks, recurring tasks, priorities) with up to **5 projects.**
    *   **AI Assistant:** Proactive AI with an allotment of **2,000 Flow Credits per month.**
    *   **Integrations:** Sync with up to **3** calendars and **1** productivity tool (e.g., Notion, Trello).
    *   **Habit Tracking:** Flexible scheduling and progress tracking.
    *   **Booking Links:** Up to **3** active public booking links.

### **Tier 3: Flow State ($11.99/month)**

*   **Price:** $11.99 per month, or $119.90 per year.
*   **Target Audience:** Power users, entrepreneurs, and busy professionals who want to maximize their productivity and automate their workflows.
*   **Goal:** Deliver an "autopilot" experience where TimeFlow handles the heavy lifting, allowing the user to stay in a state of flow.
*   **Features:**
    *   Everything in **Pro**, plus:
    *   **Core Scheduling:** **Unlimited** projects.
    *   **AI Assistant:** Autonomous AI with a generous allotment of **8,000 Flow Credits per month.**
    *   **Integrations:** **Unlimited** calendar and productivity tool integrations, plus **deep communication integration** (Gmail/Outlook).
    *   **Habit Tracking:** Gamification and detailed analytics.
    *   **Booking Links:** **Unlimited** public booking links.
    *   **Group Scheduling:** Find the best time for a group to meet.

## 3. "Flow Credits" System

"Flow Credits" are the currency used to power our advanced, API-heavy features. This system allows us to align our pricing with the underlying costs of providing these services.

### Credit Costs (per action):

*   **Simple AI Command:** 1 Credit
*   **Complex AI Command / Proactive Suggestion:** 5 Credits
*   **Third-Party Integration Sync:** 1 Credit
*   **AI Email Summarization/Drafting:** 15 Credits

### Monthly Allotments:

*   **Starter:** 200 Credits
*   **Pro:** 2,000 Credits
*   **Flow State:** 8,000 Credits

## 4. Overuse Charges

For our paid tiers, we offer a "pay-as-you-go" model for users who exceed their monthly credit allotment. This ensures their workflow is never interrupted.

*   **Pro Tier:** **$2.00 per block of 1,000 additional credits.**
*   **Flow State Tier:** **$1.50 per block of 1,000 additional credits.**
